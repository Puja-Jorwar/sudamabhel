Our Heritage

Crushing Cravings
Since 1998

What started as a tiny street corner stall in Pune has now grown into a beloved city-wide street-food brand. Sudama Bhel was built on a simple promise: to serve authentic Puneri street food with uncompromising hygiene.

In India, street food is a celebration of flavors, but concerns about hygiene often keep people away. We set out to change that. By using 100% mineral water for our pani puri, fresh hand-picked ingredients, and maintaining pristine counters, we made street food safe for everyone—from children to grandparents.

Today, our custom-blend tamarind chutneys and secret spice mixes are made in a centralized hygienic facility to ensure that a plate of Bhel in Kharadi tastes exactly as perfect as one in Chinchwad.

💧
Pure Mineral Water

We use double-filtered mineral water for all preparation and pani-puri water.

🍋
Zero Preservatives

Our spicy mint water and sweet tamarind chutneys are made fresh every single day.
